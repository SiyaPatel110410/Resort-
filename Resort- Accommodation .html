Overlooking the majestic Lake Pichola, with the Aravalli mountain range as the backdrop, The Leela Palace Udaipur oozes old-world charm and traditional Indian hospitality, with every possible modern-day amenity available. Boasting lavish decor, stylish interiors, and traditional Rajasthani artwork, guests can also expect to relax on dark-wood furniture with the daily newspaper. Additionally, an in-room Blu-Ray player and large LCD TV are available. Host meetings or large-scale banquets, indulge in an all-round royal experience at the spa, cool down in the outdoor pool, or just relax in the Jacuzzi.

The list of world-class amenities also includes a library, where guests enjoy peace and quiet. From all-day dining overlooking the lake to a walk-in wine cellar, the executive chef will ensure each and every dish is meticulously prepared and creatively served using the freshest ingredients. The 18th-century Bagore Ki Haveli is within a ten-minute walk of The Leela Palace Udaipur, as is Jagdish Temple, which is just outside the magnificent City Palace.
